Group Label

With this option, you can enter one or more titles to your columns in your applications and can change its size, its color, font and position them as desired.


Image1: Edit Cell Properties


Attributes:

  • Title - Choose the title that will appear on your Group Label.
  • Source Text - Choose the font that the title appears.
  • Font size - Set the font size of your title.
  • Horizontal alignment - Set the alignment of the title horizontally. It can be in either left or center or in the middle.
  • Vertical alignment - Setting the alignment of the title vertically. It can be below, within or on top.
  • Text Color - Choose the color of the title text that appears when the application runs.
  • Background Color - Set the background color of the space where the title appears.
