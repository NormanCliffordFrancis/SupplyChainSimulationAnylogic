View Log

This feature allows the administrator to monitor the main operations carried by ScriptCase users. The operations are displayed by Date and information such as: user login ID, user workstation IP , time, the operation type of and Action is displayed.

In the displayed calendar the days are shown in three tonalities, clear means that he didn’t have log, darkest he registers the days where he had access, and of pink color the day that this chosen team.
